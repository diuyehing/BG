# Game Pillars

## Pillar 1: Movement is Joy

The simple act of moving through the world must feel so good that traversal itself is its own reward.

## Pillar 2: Discoverable Depth

Every level contains layers that reward curiosity, from surface collectibles to hidden areas that transform how you see the world.

## Pillar 3: Player-First Storytelling

The narrative emerges from what the player does, not from what the game tells them — your journey, not ours.
